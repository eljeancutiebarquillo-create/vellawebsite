Introducing My Portfolio

Hello! I’m Aravella Angelica Balitor. This portfolio is a collection of my works, learnings, and experiences from the Quantitative Methods course. Each unit represents a different skill area, from optimization to system modeling. Through this, I hope to share not just my outputs but also how I’ve grown academically.

Unit 1: Linear Programming (Solver Example)

Case: A bakery produces bread and cakes. Each bread requires 2 hours of labor and earns ₱100 profit, while each cake requires 4 hours of labor and earns ₱250 profit. With 100 total labor hours, maximize profit.

Optimal Solution (via Excel Solver): 25 cakes, 0 bread → Profit = ₱6,250.

Unit 2: Forecasting

Example Data (Monthly demand): 120, 135, 140, 150, 160, 175

  • 3-Month Moving Average Forecast → Next = 162
  • Regression Model: Demand = 110 + 11×Month (R² = 0.95)

Midterm Project: Case Study

Case Study: A small café produces coffee (₱80 profit, 1hr prep, 2hr serving) and sandwiches (₱50 profit, 2hr prep, 1hr serving). With 40 prep hours and 30 serving hours available weekly, determine the optimal production plan.

Solution (using Solver): 10 coffee, 10 sandwiches → Profit = ₱1,300
